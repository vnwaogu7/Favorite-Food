Recipe

Time To Cook!

Yum Yum Yum

Ingrediants:

  • Tortilla/Shell
  • Ground Beef/ Ground Turkey (either/or)
  • lettuce
  • tomatos
  • Guac
  • salsa/salsa verde
  • shredded cheese
  • sour cream

Directions

  1. Grab a Saucepan or skillet. NO OIL NEEDED!!
  2. Set heat on medium high.
  3. Take your protein and put on skillet. Stir till minced. Let simmer for until all meat has browned.
  4. Warm up Shell or Tortilla (oven for shell/skillet for torilla )
  5. Drain excess oil
  6. Dice ingredients as needed (tomatoes,lettuce etc.)
  7. Assemble and Enjoy!
