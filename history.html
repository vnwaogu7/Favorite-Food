History of The Taco

Where oh where did these darn taco's come from?

It's hard to trace back the origin of the taco to one specific inventor. It's most likely due to its base components (tortilla and meat) being such a common combination. Jeffrey M. Pilcher, professor of history at the University of Minnesota believes the origin of the word was a word referring to an explosive charge made of paper wrapped around gunpowder. These devices were used to detonate silver mines. The spark in industrialization brought migrant workers throughtout the early 1900's into the US. Soon, it evolved into the dish we know and love today!
